the people who design safety equipment often have no understanding about human nature
- people will use their equipment to fulfill their [purpose], and new safety equipment means new chances to explore dangers
- sports equipment is safer with leather, as adding padding and protective shells (e.g., boxing, football) has actually made the sport MORE dangerous (i.e., aiming for the head, body as a battering ram)
- the answer is to foster an [attitude](understanding) of safety, but that requires some level of [love] for the rest of humanity, which is ALWAYS a matter of training out of the [developmental obliviousness](maturity) we all begin in
