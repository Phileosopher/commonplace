## Idols

For competitive reasons, differing idols of a resource flock together
For kinship, similar idols flock together

Career ambition is an acceptable idol

## the first sins

Adam and Eve
1 permit a temptation
2 violate a boundary
3 blame someone else
Cain
4 envy someone else
5 murder someone
6 cover up a sin
7 negotiate to save your flesh
