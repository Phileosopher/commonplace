
## prayer list

Pray for our nation and government

1. Revival in the Church
2. The hearts of people to awaken to God
3. The Church to be a light and a witness to the people
4. Our leaders will continue to allow us to live in tranquility and dignity
5. Wisdom for all those in government
6. Our leaders at each level of government: Federal, State, County, City
7. The Christians in government to be strong and courageous

Pray for the Church

8. The elders to have wisdom and strength
9. Protection for elders and their families
10. The deacons
11. The Sunday School teachers and youth group leaders
12. The worship team
13. Our witness to the neighbors and those who visit
14. Unity and forbearance
15. Boldness, faith, and strength
16. The gospel will spread, and the harvest of souls be plentiful
17. Christians who are facing persecution
