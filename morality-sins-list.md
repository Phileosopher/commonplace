
# List of sins according to the Bible

The Ten Commandments:

1. Letting anything or anyone but God receive our worship ([Exodus 20:1-3](https://biblehub.com/bsb/exodus/20.htm))
2. Making any image an object of worship ([Exodus 20:4-6](https://biblehub.com/bsb/exodus/20.htm))
3. Misusing the name of God (profaning) or blaspheming His character or reputation ([Exodus 20:7](https://biblehub.com/exodus/20-7.htm))
4. Failing to keep one day a week and one year out of seven for rest and worship ([Exodus 20:8-11](https://biblehub.com/bsb/exodus/20.htm))
5. Failing to honor parents in speech, thoughts, or deeds ([Exodus 20:12](https://biblehub.com/exodus/20-12.htm))
6. Taking human life beyond administering God's justice ([Exodus 20:13](https://biblehub.com/exodus/20-13.htm))
7. Having sexual relationships outside of marriage ([Exodus 20:14](https://biblehub.com/exodus/20-14.htm))
8. Taking property, time, or benefit that isn't rightfully ours ([Exodus 20:15](https://biblehub.com/exodus/20-15.htm))
9. Creating a false impression by action, statement, or omission ([Exodus 20:16](https://biblehub.com/exodus/20-16.htm))
10. Desiring another's property, spouse, or employees ([Exodus 20:17](https://biblehub.com/exodus/20-17.htm))

- Divorce and remarriage ([Malachi 2:14-16](https://biblehub.com/bsb/malachi/2.htm), [Luke 16:18](https://biblehub.com/luke/16-18.htm))
- Plotting evil against another ([Zechariah 8:17](https://biblehub.com/zechariah/8-17.htm))
- Religious observance without a pure and sincere heart ([Isaiah 1:13](https://biblehub.com/isaiah/1-13.htm))
- Condemning the just and justifying the wicked ([Proverbs 17:15](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/17-15.htm))
- Pride in the heart ([Proverbs 16:5](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/16-5.htm))
- Lying lips ([Proverbs 12:22](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/12-22.htm))
- Sowing discord ([Proverbs 6:19](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/6-19.htm))
- Making trouble ([Proverbs 6:18](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/6-18.htm))
- Looking proud ([Proverbs 6:16-17](https://biblehub.com/bsb/proverbs/6.htm))
- Loving violence ([Psalm 11:5](https://biblehub.com/psalms/11-5.htm))
- Cheating others ([Deuteronomy 25:13-16](https://biblehub.com/bsb/deuteronomy/25.htm))
- Exploiting or not aiding the poor ([Psalm 109:15-16](https://biblehub.com/bsb/psalms/109.htm))
- Engaging in occult practices ([Deuteronomy 18:10-11](https://biblehub.com/bsb/deuteronomy/18.htm))

Sexual sins:

- Sex among singles ([1 Corinthians 6:18](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/6-18.htm))

Some things are always sinful:

- Charms, incantations, spells
- Enchantments - using the human voice or music to bring someone under psychic control
- Sorcerers, witches, enchanters, wizards
- Dealing with evil spirits or using magic (witchcraft or wizardry)
- Using power from controlling or getting aid from evil spirits (sorcery)
- Fortune-telling, divination, astrology/stargazing, soothsaying, prognostication
- Communicating with the dead and conjuring spirits of the dead to predict the future or to influence events (necromancy)

Many things are philosophical gateways for practices God has forbidden

- extra-sensory perception (ESP)
- Spirit guides
- Out-of-body experiences, astral projection
- Shamanism, psychic surgery, crystal healing
- Spoon bending, trance channeling, pyramid power
- Discussion of a one-world government or a unified world (often mixed with [climate change](politics-leftism.md))
- Ouija boards, many horror movies

Many practices aren't inherently evil but straddle a *very* fine line:

- Martial arts, yoga
- New age music, symbolism
- Brain stimulation devices, biofeedback
- Relaxation techniques, flotation tanks, regression therapy
- Value clarification, visualization, Law of Attraction
- Transcendental meditation, self-hypnosis
- Horoscopes
- Entertainment like video games and cartoons with spiritual content or "alternate life" themes
