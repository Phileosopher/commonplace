
# Logical proofs of God's existence

Kalam argument

1. Everything started with something.
2. God is that something that started everything.

Liebniz argument

1. Everything has an explanation for it.
2. Therefore, God explains the universe's existence.

Fine-Tuning argument

1. The universe is highly ordered and necessary for [life](science-life.md) to exist.
2. Something must have fine-tuned it.
3. It's absurd to presume it came from pure randomness.
4. God was the fine-tuner.

Moral argument

1. [Morality](morality.md) requires God to exist.
2. Morals do exist.
3. God exists

- This argument is very old (first proposed by Socrates in his "Euthyphro").
- It also happens to also nullify the [Evil God Challenge](philosophy-paradoxes.md).

Ontological argument

1. There's something that's the "greatest" to compare everything to.
2. That comparison is God.
