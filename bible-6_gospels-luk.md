
# Luke commentary

## 3:23-38

Besides the numerology, there is meaning in the cascade of names.

- Luke 3:23-38 captures the father's side, while Matthew 1 captures the mother's.
- Luke starts at Joseph and works upward.

Now, from the top:

- The increase and climbing up is the gift of Yahweh, with my counsel/king joined to destroy.
- The increase is the gift of Yahweh, a weighty comfort specially maintained by God, illuminating while made small.
- The gift of Yahweh is famous and increases, and is praiseworthy and favored by Jehovah's will.
- I have asked the seed of Babylon of God, and the light of Yah is my counsel/king, adornment, and oracle.
- The people of God are watchers, and Yahweh is to be exalted as their salvation and help.
- The gift of Yahweh has joined in hearing His praise and increase.
- God will establish a dove, a vibrant enchanter who is the given, loving, unique, serving gift of Yahweh.
- He is a temple pillar, a perfectly rewarding enchanter of the people who chose.
- He is elevated beyond our comprehension, and is a courtyard that breaks out against death.
- Let Him be praised as the replacer who laughs as an exalted father.
- His breath is hot and dry, and His layered friendship causes division.
- His anger swiftly passes with a petition, and He is the purchaser that releases and heals.
- His name is consolation, and He is made low as the man of the dart.
- He has a disciplined command, and is the praise of God.
- He is the purchaser of fallible and weak man, and places the people of the dirt with God.
