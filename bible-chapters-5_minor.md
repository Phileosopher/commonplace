
# Bible chapter summary 5: Minor prophets

## Hosea

1. Hosea's wife and children
2. Israel's adultery rebuked, God's mercy to Israel
3. Hosea redeems his wife
4. God's case against His people
5. Israel and Judah's judgment
6. Israel and Judah's unrepentance
7. Ephraim's sin
8. Israel will reap the whirlwind
9. Israel's punishment
10. retribution for Israel's sin
11. out of Egypt I called My son, God's love for Israel
12. reproof of Ephraim, Judah, and Jacob
13. God's anger against Israel, Israel's death and resurrection, judgment on Samaria
14. a call to repentance, a promise of God's blessing

## Joel

1. the invasion of locusts, a call to mourning, a call to repent
2. the army of locusts, return with all your heart, restoration promised, God will pour out His Spirit
3. God judges the nations, blessings for God's people

## Amos

1. judgment on Israel's neighbors
2. judgment on Moab, Judah, and Israel
3. witnesses summoned against Israel
4. God's punishment hasn't brought repentance
5. lamentation against Israel, a call to repent, woe to Israel
6. woe to those at ease in Zion, the pride of Israel
7. locusts, fire, and the plumb line, Amaziah accuses Amos and he defends himself
8. the basket of summer fruit and God's famine
9. the destruction of Israel and a promise of restoration

## Obadiah

1. Edom will be destroyed, Israel will be delivered

## Jonah

1. Jonah is called and flees God, the great storm, Jonah is cast into the sea
2. Jonah's prayer
3. the Ninevites repent
4. Jonah's anger at God's compassion

## Micah

1. the coming judgment, weeping and mourning
2. woe to the oppressors, reproof of false prophets, the remnant of Israel
3. the rulers and prophets are condemned
4. the mountain of the house of the Lord, the restoration of Zion
5. a ruler from Bethlehem, the remnant of Jacob
6. the case against Israel, the punishment of Israel
7. Israel's great misery, Israel's confession and comfort, God's compassion on Israel

## Nahum

1. the burden against Nineveh
2. the overthrow of Nineveh
3. God's judgment against Nineveh
