
# Psalms' target audience

## 1

For the upright in contrast to the wicked

- Blessed is the man who does not walk in the counsel of the wicked.

## 2

Against those who reject Christ

- Why do the nations rage and the peoples plot in vain?

## 3

For the upright and undefended

- Deliver me, O Lord!

## 4

For the publicly shamed

- Answer me when I call, O God of my righteousness!

## 5

For the upright against liars

- Give ear to my words, O Lord; consider my groaning.

## 6

For the repentant

- O Lord, do not rebuke me in Your anger or discipline me in Your wrath.

## 7

For the upright against oppressive enemies

- O Lord my God, I take refuge in You; save me and deliver me from all my pursuers...

## 8

Direct praise

- O Lord, our Lord, how majestic is Your name in all the earth!
