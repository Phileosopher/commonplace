
# Engineering glossary

Baffle - a narrow, vertical barrier in a container of liquid designed to keep the liquid from sloshing around from movement

Compression - broad term for pressure applied to an object

Gasket - a seal between two mating surfaces, typically for preventing leakage while under compression

Mating - broad term for a direct connection between two things

Static - not moving

Viscosity - how much friction liquids have inside themselves (e.g., engine oil versus water)
