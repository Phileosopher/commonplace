
# Bible chapter summary 7: the Historical book

## Acts

1. prologue, the ascension, Matthias replaces Judas
2. the Holy Spirit at Pentecost, Peter addresses the crowd, three thousand believe, the fellowship of believers
3. lame man walks, Peter speaks to the onlookers
4. Peter and John before Sanhedrin, the name of Jesus forbidden, the believers' prayer, sharing among believers
5. Ananias and Sapphira, apostles heal many, apostles arrested and freed, apostles before Sanhedrin, Gamaliel's advice
6. choosing of the seven, arrest of Stephen
7. Stephen's address: the call of Abraham, Joseph sold into Egypt, Israel oppressed in Egypt, birth and adoption of Moses, rejection and flight of Moses, call of Moses, rebellion of Israel, and the tabernacle of the testimony, the stoning of Stephen
8. Saul persecutes the Church, Philip in Samaria, Simon the sorcerer, Philip and the Ethiopian
9. road to Damascus, Ananias baptizes Saul, Saul preaches at Damascus, escape from Damascus, Saul in Jerusalem, healing of Aeneas, raising of Tabitha
10. Cornelius sends for Peter, Peter's vision, Peter called to Caesarea, Peter visits Cornelius, good news for the Gentiles, gentiles receive the Holy Spirit
11. Peter's report at Jerusalem, church at Antioch
12. James killed and Peter imprisoned, Peter rescued, death of Herod
13. Paul begins first missionary journey, miracle at Cyprus, Gospel shared in Pisidian Antioch, light for the Gentiles
14. Paul and Barnabas at Iconium, visit to Lystra and Derbe, strengthening the disciples
15. dispute over circumcision, council at Jerusalem, letter to the Gentile believers, believers at Antioch rejoice, Paul begins second missionary journey
16. Timothy joins Paul and Silas, Paul's vision of the Macedonians, Lydia's conversion in Philippi, Paul and Silas imprisoned, the conversion of the jailer, an official apology
17. uproar in Thessalonica, character of the Bereans, Paul in Athens, Paul's address to the Areopagus
18. Paul ministers in Corinth, Paul before Gallio, Paul returns to Antioch, Paul begins third missionary journey
19. Holy Spirit received at Ephesus, Paul ministers in Ephesus, botched exorcism, riot in Ephesus
20. Paul in Macedonia and Greece, Eutychus revived at Troas, from Troas to Miletus, Paul's farewell to the Ephesians
21. Paul's journey to Jerusalem, Paul visits Philip the evangelist, Paul arrives in Jerusalem, Paul seizeda th Temple, Paul addresses the crowd
22. Paul's defense to the crowd
23. Paul before the Sanhedrin, the plot to kill Paul, Paul sent to Felix
24. Tertullus prosecutes Paul, Paul's defense to Felix, verdict postponed
25. Paul's trial before Festus, Paul appeals to Caesar, Festus consults Agrippa, Paul before Agrippa and Bernice
26. Paul's testimony to Agrippa, Festus interrupts Paul's defense
27. Paul sails for Rome, the storm at sea, the shipwreck
28. ashore on Malta, Paul arrives in Italy, Paul preaches at Rome
